Dynamics of Income Inequality in Chile

This project takes a look at the dynamics of income inequality in Chile throughout the course of democratization over the past few decades. This study uses data gatered from the Luxemburg Income Study (LIS) from 1990 until 2015. The data is treated using STATA and formatting follows the conventions laid out in recent monograph literature.
